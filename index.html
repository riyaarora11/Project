Welcome to Doon Group Of Colleges Saharanpur Uttar pradesh (India)-:

Doon Group Of Colleges, one of the premier Institutions in the field of Technical and Management Education, was established in 2002. The institute is located at Dehradun Road Saharanpur (U.P) India in the National Capital region . The institute is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, Ministry of HRD, Government of India and is affiliated to the Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. In a short span of time the institute has grown from strength to strength and is counted among the best engineering colleges in Northern India.

Placements-:

The Institute will give special attention to Training and Placements of its students. For this purpose a full time T&P officer will shortly be appointed. The Institute has made a clear roadmap for making its students more employable by way of developing their overall personality.The teaching pedagogy adopted by the institute, regular industrial visits, regular guest lectures delivered by experts from industry will make our students better from the rest of the B-School graduates, are some of the steps that will be taken by the Institute right from the start.
